About Us

Founded in 2023, Envision Studios was built on a simple idea: to inspire creativity, storytelling, and fun through lego videos for kids and families. We believe that every brick has a story to tell — and we’re here to bring those stories to life through animation, adventure, and imagination.

At Envision Studios, we combine a little creativity and a lot of fun to create inspiring videos.

Our Mission

To build a world, piece by piece, where creativity, imagination, and fun click together — encouraging kids to dream, design, and discover.

The Process

  • Creativity: Every video starts with a simple idea and grows into something incredible.
  • Imagination: During the process of creating a video, we let our imaginations go wild.
  • Fun: We keep the video full of inspiring content designed to build each other up.

Join the Adventure

Whether you’re a young builder, a parent, or just a lego fan at heart — there’s a place for you here. Imagine with us as we build the future of storytelling, one brick at a time.
